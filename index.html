My name is Pau Ramos, I obtained my Ph.D. in Astrophysics in 2020 [during a global Pandemic, mind you]  and currently working at the National Observatory of Japan, where I keep exploring the dynamics of the stellar disc of our Galaxy, the Milky Way.

My research interests comprise, but not limited to,

  • Galactic dynamics: disc evolution, its response to perturbations and coupling
  • Kinematic substructure, its origin and couping to large scale features
  • Milky Way accretion history
