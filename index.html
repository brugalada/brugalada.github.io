「 As long as there's music, I'll keep on dancing 」 

My name is Pau Ramos, I obtained my Ph.D. in Astrophysics in 2020 [during a global Pandemic, mind you]  and currently employed by the Observatory of Strasbourg to keep exploring the dynamics of the stellar disc of our Galaxy, the Milky Way.

My research interests comprise, but not limited to,

  • Galactic dynamics: disc evolution, its response to perturbations, coupling with halo
  • Kinematic substructure, its origin and couping to large scale features
  • Milky Way accretion history
